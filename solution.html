Solution

The light and heat of the sun, the movement of the water and wind, the carbohydrates in plants, and the warmth in the Earth are all sources in which can supply energy sustainably. There are many technologies that are used to convert each of these resources into electricity. Each comes with a set of challenges and benifits. However, together they represent our best hope in fighting climate change. Solar-power provides energy from the sun and can supply a siginificant portion of global electricity needs. Wind turbines harness air currents and convert them into emission free power. Although wind power is not being used dramatically, the potentiall that it has is huge and is expected to grow in the coming years. Energy storage, although it isn't a source itself is a critical component in creating low-carbon electriicty system. Having the ability to hold the energy and use it whenever is needed will allow the transition to renewable energy sources to occur smoothly and easily. Although there are many other technologies that could be used to create a zero emission world, these alone would reduce a significant portion of the worlds carbon emissions.
